# OPNMARK

**Find the mark. Build toward it.**

OPNMARK turns job descriptions into clear, actionable plans for stronger applications.

Open a role to understand what matters, see how your experience aligns, identify what is missing, and build the proof needed to move forward.

## What it does

Most career tools reduce candidates to a score. OPNMARK takes a more useful approach: it translates each opportunity into a structured workspace built around requirements, evidence, gaps, and next actions.

OPNMARK helps you:

- Separate meaningful requirements from generic job-description noise
- Connect role requirements to evidence from your experience
- Identify gaps without reducing your candidacy to an arbitrary score
- Generate a practical list of what to build, learn, rewrite, or demonstrate
- Create focused application materials grounded in the actual role
- Track opportunities and maintain context throughout the application process

## Workflow

### 01 — Open

Import a job listing and convert it into a structured role brief.

### 02 — Mark

Identify the skills, responsibilities, experience, and evidence defining a strong candidate.

### 03 — Match

Connect those requirements to relevant work from your résumé, portfolio, projects, and broader experience.

### 04 — Build

Create a prioritized plan for closing gaps and strengthening your candidacy.

### 05 — Apply

Prepare tailored résumé content, application responses, outreach, and interview material.

## Core concepts

### Role Brief

A clear interpretation of what the company is actually hiring for.

### The Mark

The requirements and evidence that define a credible candidate for the role.

### Current Proof

The experience, accomplishments, and work you can already demonstrate.

### Build List

The projects, skills, case studies, revisions, and materials that would make your application stronger.

### Application Kit

Focused application assets generated from the role and your verified experience.

## Principles

- **Evidence over confidence theater**
- **Clear actions over arbitrary scores**
- **Specific context over generic career advice**
- **Constructive gaps over automatic rejection**
- **Human judgment supported by AI—not replaced by it**

## Privacy

Your résumé and career information belong to you.

OPNMARK is being designed to minimize unnecessary data retention and to clearly explain how submitted information is processed. Personal career data will not be sold or used to train public models.

## Status

OPNMARK is currently in active development.

The product was previously known as **Aptdex** and is being rebuilt around a broader career workflow: understanding opportunities, identifying the mark, and building toward them.
